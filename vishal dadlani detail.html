Vishal Dadlani

Born : 28 June 1973
Years active : 1999–present
About :

Vishal Dadlani is an eminent music director in B-town and generally composes music with partner Shekhar Ravjiani. Vishal started his career in the industry in 1999 and scored the music for ‘Pyaar Mein Kabhi Kabhi’, which became quite popular among music listeners. And then came along ‘Jhankaar Beats’, which proved to be a major turning point of his career. The songs of this film became chartbusters, and his efforts were materialized when he won the R D Burman New Music Talent Award along with Shekhar.


Career :

The duo rose to prominence when they composed the score for the film Jhankaar Beats which included the song "Tu Aashiqui Hai". They won the Filmfare RD Burman Award for New Music Talent for Jhankaar Beats. The music for the film Musafir combined techno music with Indian sounds.[citation needed] The score included "Saaki" and "Door Se Paas". In 2005 they composed the scores for three films: Salaam Namaste, Dus and Bluffmaster.

Vishal Dadlani is also the vocalist of Mumbai-based electronic band Pentagram. He collaborated with Imogen Heap on the song "Minds Without Fear" for an episode of The Dewarists.

Shekhar Ravjiani is a trained classical singer (under Ustad Niaz Ahmed Khan). He learned to play the accordion from his father, a music enthusiast, Hasmukh Ravjiani. Shekhar was a participant of Zee TV singing contest Sa Re Ga Ma Pa in 1997. He composed and sung Marathi songs "Saazni" in 2012 and "Saavli" (with Sunidhi Chauhan) in 2013.


Personal Life :

Vishal Dadlani is an Indian singer, music director, vocalist, actor and one of the duos Vishal - Shekhar team of music directors and have given music in Hindi and Marathi films.

Vishal is the vocalist for the band Pentagram, and has created a great fusion of music mixing techno with Indian sound effects. The duo have given super hit music in films like Jhankaar Beats, Kaante, Pyaar Mein Kabhi Kabhi, Salaam Namaste, Bluffmaster, Golmaal, Om Shanti Om, Dostana, Don, Tees Maar Khan, Ra. One, Rascals, The Dirty Picture, Student Of The Year and many other films.

Vishal has given play back singing in films like Race, Student Of The Year, Break Ke Baad, Krazzy 4, Tashan, Ishaqzaade, Jhoom Barabar Jhoom, I Hate Luv Storys, Zindagi Na Milegi Dobara, Ladies Vs Ricky Bahl and many other films.

Vishal and Shekhar were judges on the musical reality show- Amul Star Voice Of India Mummy Ki Superstars, and mentors and judges on music reality show Sa Re Ga Ma Pa on Zee TV.
