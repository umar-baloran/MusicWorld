Honey Sing

Born : 15 March 1983
Years active : 2004–present
About :

Honey Sing better known by his stage name Yo Yo Honey Singh, is an Indian rapper, music producer, singer and film actor. He started as a session and recording artist, and became a Bhangra producer. He has also begun to produce music for Bollywood films.


Career :

Honey Singh started his singing career and always prefer to sing in his mother tongue Punjabi and Hindi. He is avoid to sing in English that’s why he is most popular in Punjabi Music Industry.

Honey Singh given his rap in many Punjabi Songs from last few years. Diljit Dosanjh’s Song “Lak 28 Kudi Da” was released in 2011 and Honey Singh given his rap. This was the promotional track of Punjab film Lion of Punjab and reached at number 1 position BBC Asian Download Charts of May, 2011.

Honey Singh first music album “International Villager” was released in Nov, 2011 and one track “Gabru” from “International Villager” which was sung by “J-Star” got Top Position on Asian Music Charts. He is sung for many Punjabi Songs and people love, Honey Singh by his rapping style and lyrics.

Honey Singh sung “Lungi Dance” in SRK film Chennai Express and got nominated in Big Star Entertainment Awards for Most Entertaining Singer (Male).


Personal Life :

As we have mention above that Honey Singh Born in Hoshiarpur, Punjab in 1983. He is completed his some part of school time from Guru Nanak Public School in Punjab Bagh after that he went to Trinity School, London where he studied about music and completed his study.

Honey Singh living single in his life, but some news was came that he is married. Some pictures come in news where he sitting with bride by wearing a turban. Honey Singh denied this news and said that he is not married and it’s not a real wedding. Some days back, he admitted to have been married.
