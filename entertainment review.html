Music Review : Entertainment
Director :  Farhad-Sajid
Music :  Sachin-Jigar
Lyrics :  Mayur Puri, Priya Panchal and Ashish Pandit
Starring :  Akshay Kumar, Tamannaah Bhatia, Krishna Abhishek, Johnny Lever, Sonu Sood , Prakash Raj and Mithun Chakraborty

July 25, 2014 9:53:07 AM IST

By Rafat, Glamsham Editorial

One does expect that writers Sajid-Farhad's (of HOUSEFULL fame) debut directorial venture under one of India's prestigious film banners, Tips Music Films, should boast of some chartbusting numbers. With young and talented composers, Sachin-jigar, a regular for Tips Music films, at the helm of affairs, hopes are raised even higher. So let's check out whether the audio has something entertaining to offer.

The celebration number, Punjabi style, 'Veerey Di Wedding' which celebrates the wedding of the one of the lead protagonist, a dog (!) is a routine Bhangra number made slightly above ordinary due to Mika's zestful singing.

Broken vocals, DJ scratches and heightened tempo and rhythmic beats all make for an excellent remix of 'Veerey Di Wedding'.

Tips and Atif Aslam have had a hundred percent success record and 'Tera Naam Doon' is sure to carry the good, in fact excellent work forward. The superb sufi love ballad has it all that will make this number a sure shot chartbuster with an easy to hum, infectious, fresh tune, excellent renditions (both Atif and Shalmali) and lovely arrangements.

Jigar, Priya Panchal and Madhav Krishna seem to having the time of their life in this superb zany hip hop party number, 'Johnny Johnny'. The extremely hilarious lyrics are the highlight of the track, and one does look forward to seeing this number on the big screen.

It's veteran singer, Udit Narayan who enthralls all with his no holds barred rendition of the mast dance number, 'Teri Mahima Aprampaar', along with Anushka Manchanda. The extremely catchy tune and lyrics are another highlight.

It's Atif's turn again to woo with the soft, breezy love cum separation ballad, 'Nahin Woh Saamne'. This brief number (which may or may not be a part of the album) impresses in all departments, especially Atif's almost unplugged and flawless rendition.

The audio of ENTERTAINMENT is surely entertaining and Sachin-Jigar do offer (as always) something fresh and full time pass songs that gel well with the script of the movie. Our favourites are 'Tera Naam Doon' and 'Johnny Johnny' for its fun flavor. The audio should ensure a good to decent opening for ENTERTAINMENT when it releases all over on 8th August, 2014.
