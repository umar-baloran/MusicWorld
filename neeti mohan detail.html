Neeti Mohan

Born : 13 Nov 1985
Years active : 2003–present
About :

Neeti Mohan also credited as Neeti, is an Indian popular singer. She was one of the winners of the Channel V show Popstars and as such, became a member of the Indian pop group Aasma with the other winners of the show.


Career :

Neeti Mohan is the stopper beauty who has credited awards in his account for the profile of playback singer. She initiated her career as contestant of the reality show “popstar” that was aired on the channel V that rendered her a singing platform with the launch of a musical band named “Aasma” that comprised of the members who had won the contest along with her. She even participated in a different musical platform offered by Star plus via a reality show named as “Music Ka Maha Mugabla” in which she was trained under the guideline of Shankar Mahadevan especial team named as “Shankar’s Rockstar”. She after such platform made her attempts to reach the bollywood as an actress and went for the audition of “Socha Na Tha” one of the Dharmendra production house movie. Finally, she was rendered the opportunity to do the playback singing for the song “Ishq Wala Love” of the movie “Student of the Year” that was released in the year 2013. This song offered her exposure in the music and with this she started receiving one after another.


Personal Life :

She is an alumnus of Delhi's Miranda House, a philosophy graduate and a dance instructor as well. She was also a NCC cadet during her college days. She did her schooling from prestigious Birla Balika Vidyapeeth, Pilani, Rajasthan.

She is the daughter of Brijmohan and Kusum, and is the eldest in the family of four talented sisters. Her sister Shakti Mohan is the winner of Dance India Dance on Zee TV, and her other sister Mukti Mohan was part of the winning team "Masakali Girls" on the dance show Zara Nachke Dikha on Star Plus. She also has a sister Kriti Mohan.
